Badrinath: The Sacred Temple of Lord Vishnu Nestled in the majestic Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, Badrinath is one of the holiest and most revered pilgrimage sites in India. Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, this sacred town is home to the famed Badrinath Temple, which is a central part of the Char Dham Yatra, a pilgrimage circuit that includes Yamunotri, Gangotri, and Kedarnath. Badrinath, with its rich history, divine significance, and breathtaking natural beauty, attracts millions of devotees from across the globe who come to seek blessings, enlightenment, and spiritual solace.
